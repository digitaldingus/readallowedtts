# 🔊 ReadAllowed TTS (Google Chrome Edition)

High-performance Text-to-Speech (TTS) assistant designed for the **Antigravity** developer suite for seamless, barrier-free content reading. Just highlight any text, click the popup, and listen comfortably without clutter.


---

## ✨ Key Features
- **Highlight to Speak**: Instant dictation of any selected web text.
- **Custom Voice Picker**: Select your preferred synthesis voice from all installed Chrome & OS system voices.
- **Dynamic Speech Rates**: linear Slider support to speed up or slow down dictation on the fly.
- **Elective Assistive Mode**: Invisible ARIA live-regions push prompt confirmations for full screen reader user compatibility.

---

## 🛠️ Free Local Installation (Load Unpacked)

Because this is distributed as a free utility, you can load and run it fully without navigating paywalled app-store steps:

1.  Download or unzip the **`read-allowed-chrome-v1.2.0.zip`** bundle to a folder on your computer.
2.  Open Google Chrome and navigate to **`chrome://extensions/`**.
3.  In the top-right corner, toggle **Developer mode** to ON.
4.  Click the **"Load unpacked"** button in the top-left section.
5.  Select the extracted `chrome` folder you just unzipped.

---

## ⚙️ How to Use & Settings
1. Click the **Puzzle Piece** Icon in your Chrome toolbar, and pin **ReadAllowed**.
2. **Highlight** any paragraph or sentence on a webpage.
3. Click the **ReadAllowed** icon in your toolbar, then press **📖 Speak Selection**!
4. Configure or switch your Narrator’s Speed and Voice choices anytime from that exact popup view.
